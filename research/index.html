Research

My research seeks the use of blockchain technology to address data regulation concerns, such as the presents in GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) in Europe and the Brazilian Data Protection Law (Law n. 13.709/2018 - Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados Pessoais - LGPD). So, I have studied the LGPD application in the public health and education environment to identify the data subjects' rights and the data controllers' and processors' duties. To do so, my research group is developing an ontology to clarify the roles and entities involved. Furthermore, we have found that a data governance model is essential to companies' conformance with LGPD. In this sense, permissioned blockchain platforms are potential models to support LGPD data compliance. Therefore, we have studied the Hyperledger Fabric platform resources to define data governance in practice.

This project aims to study how the permissioned blockchain technology can suport such data regulation. Furthermore, we are comparing diffrent data regulations, e.g., GDPR and Canadian data protection in regards to consent management in public health researches. After that, our roadmap considers the study of data governace in the educational sector, considering the privacy by design principles.
